What is a Soapberry

A cousin of the Lychee, the soapberry tree produces the small leathery-skinned fruit that contains saponin, a natural substance that creates its soap-like quality. These berries have been valued for its anti-bacterial, medicinal, and dermatological benefits in various regions of tropical and sub-tropical Asia since ancient times. When rubbed in your hands, the fruit forms a rich, natural lather and an eye-opening reconnection with nature – it's time to stop using those harmful chemicals!

Shampoo / Body Gel

Once a year, our family farmers wild-harvest the soapberries from the tropical forests of southern Taiwan. Through an exclusive method for extracting the active interface saponin from the soapberry fruit, we obtain the highest quality concentrate of the berry's natural cleaning power. Then, we use our extract to produce a variety of gentle, natural, non-toxic body care products. We will initially offer our own soapberry shampoo, shower gel, and hand soap, with plans to create a whole suite of natural body care products! With every purchase of our refined products, you'll receive a few raw soapberries to keep your hands clean the original way. Just soak them a little in water, and rub them with your hands to get a creamy, cleansing lather.
