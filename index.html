What is a Soapberry

A cousin of the Lychee, soapberry's leathery skin is rich of saponin, a natural surfactant that makes it the nature's perfect cleansing gift. Before chemical cleaners were introduced, the soapberry was the preferred method of cleaning. These berries have been valued for its anti-bacterial, medicinal, and dermatological benefits in various regions of sub-tropical Asia since ancient times.

When rubbed in your hands, the fruit forms a rich, creamy lather that reconnects you with nature

Shampoo / Body Gel

Once a year, our family farmers wild-harvest the soapberries from the tropical forests of southern Taiwan. Through an exclusive and eco-friendly method for extracting the active interface saponin from soapberries, we obtain the highest quality concentrate of its natural cleaning power. Then, we use our extract to produce a variety of gentle, natural, non-toxic soapberry shampoo, shower gel, and eventually a whole suite of natural body care treasures.
